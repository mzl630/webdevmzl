Zachary David Alexander "Zac" Efron

(born October 18, 1987)is an American actor and singer. He began acting professionally in the early 2000's, and became known as a teen idol after his lead roles in the Disney's Channel High School Musical, the WB series Summerland Summerland, and the 2007 film version of the Broadway musical Hairspray. Efron has since starred in the films 17 Again, Me and Orson Welles, Charlie St. Cloud, New Year's Eve, The Lucky One, That Awkward Moment, Neighbors, and The Lorax.
